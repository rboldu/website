I am a Ph.D. Candidate at the Augmented Human Lab (AHLAB) , The University of Auckland, advised by As.Prof. Suranga Nanayakkara.

I develop embedded systems that bring computing and interactivity closer to users and empower them to naturally interact with the environment. I like to build high fidelity prototypes that work outside the laboratory environment.

This has driven me to join manufacturing hardware acceleration programs such as Brinc.io (Hong Kong/Shenzen) and business strategies programs such as Lean Launch Program from NUS Enterprise (Singapore). I have been part of some of the top international research institutes, such as Fluid Interfaces , at MIT Media Lab, and led high social impact projects such as creating a wearable device to assist people that has visual impairments (FingerReader)

I graduated with a B.Tech (Hons.) in Electronics and Telecomunications from Ramon University - La Salle Bonanova, Barcelona, Spain in 2015. For more details, check my CV or send me an email.
